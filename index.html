LASTEST SPACE NEWS 2024

NASA’s New Hybrid Rocket Engine:

Engineers at NASA’s Marshall Space Flight Center have unveiled a new low-cost hybrid rocket engine testbed. This innovative engine features variable flow capability and a 20-second continuous burn duration, aimed at enhancing future space propulsion systems​.

Perseid Meteor Shower:

The Perseid meteor shower, one of the most anticipated meteor showers of the year, is set to peak around August 12-13. This meteor shower, caused by debris from the comet Swift-Tuttle, will be best viewed in the Northern Hemisphere. Observers are advised to find a dark location away from city lights and to be patient as the meteors streak across the sky at about 37 miles per second​.

Chandrayaan-4 Update:

India plans to launch Chandrayaan-4 in 2028 to bring back lunar soil samples.
